CodeSLAM at Uplift Summit Fall 2017

On Saturday morning of October 28, 2017 at Uplift Summit Preparatory, scholars were challenged to build a social media profile for their favorite celebrity or fictional character using HTML & CSS.
The Uplift Scholars had 2 hours to complete this challenge, and although the majority of them had no previous experience coding in these languages (HTML & CSS), the Uplift scholars had a great time and they created web pages showcased below.

Scholars included in this challenge attend the following schools: Uplift Summit, Uplift Grand, Uplift Mighty, Uplift North Hills
